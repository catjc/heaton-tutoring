Welcome

My name is Catherine Powell. I am a fully qualified primary teacher offering tutoring to children and young people in the Newcastle upon Tyne area. I currently tutor children of all ages in primary school and within Key Stage 3 in secondary school. Please read on to find out more...

Tutoring and School

Every child deserves the very best education. Schools and teachers work hard to offer this to all children in their care. However, school life can be fast paced and busy. Tutoring can give your child the individual attention they need at a pace that suits them. This personalised support can really work wonders. Tutoring will improve your child's confidence and help them make great progress.

Professional Experience

  • Extensive experience supporting children 1:1 and working with children with a variety of special educational needs, such as dyslexia.
  • BA(Hons) Biological Sciences (Oxford University); PGCE in Primary Education (Newcastle University)
  • Maths, Chemistry and Biology A Levels

Personal Support

I offer personalised support tailored to your child's individual needs in all areas of the curriculum, specialising in mathematics and English. Individual attention by a qualified teacher means your child can focus on their key areas. With supportive tutoring, your child will really blossom and grow.

Tutoring Sessions - Primary

As an experienced primary school teacher, I have in depth knowledge of the school curriculum from Year 1 to Year 6. This enables me to accurately assess your child's learning needs. I am able to plan personalised learning activities to build on your child's strengths and correct any misconceptions. My sessions aim to develop your child's love of learning and foster independence.

Tutoring Sessions - Secondary

When tutoring young people who are at secondary school, I put a strong focus on helping students to develop their problem solving skills, empowering them to help themselves. Through primary-based teaching, I have a strong understanding of the areas where earlier misconceptions can arise and how these can continue to cause difficulties for students in making progress at secondary school. I am able to draw on my wide subject knowledge to support my tutoring.
